Privacy Policy

R.K. Loudwire's YouTube Auto Liker does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

What This Extension Does

This extension operates entirely within your browser and only interacts with YouTube pages to automatically like videos you are already watching when the video reaches the 50% playtime mark.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected.
No browsing data is stored.
No information is sent to external servers.
No Logins Are Detected Or Stored.
We do not read cookies that we did not create for the future "Total Videos Liked" feature.

Third Parties

This extension does not communicate with any external services or third parties. At All. Ever.
